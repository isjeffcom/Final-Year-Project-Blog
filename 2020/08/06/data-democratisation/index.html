data_democratisation

There is a concept name the democratisation of knowledge or in a more modern term the data democratisation. I am always thinking about the purpose of the data visualisation during my study. I wonder if that is more straightforward and natural to tell the final result to the decision making endpoint rather than presenting data visualisation. Plantly speaking, what is the point to go around all these troubles instead of display a simple advise, which we might think that is the final goal for developing the technology. The data democratisation might be the concept to justify the purpose of data visualisation.

In the modern world, through the development of communication technology, people are more involved in the political, whether in a democratic country or not. And there is usually a cognition gap existed between the professionals/scientists to the policy-making groups. This gap created a living space for misinformation, false news and chaotic data sharing. Hence it could cause the government to miss or fail to resolve real problems. To deliver the data and knowledge directly to all the decision making groups or the general public, and create an environment that allows them to experience the potential outcomes, might be a way to fill this gap.

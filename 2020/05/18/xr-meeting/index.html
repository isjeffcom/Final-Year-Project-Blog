A glance of VR/AR meeting

AR/VR/XR meeting is an interesting development throughout the global epidemic. Most of the industry needs a way to enhance their remote working experience. Can people use what they have on their hand to build up an XR reality enhancement meeting instead of a video meeting? What will be changed?

This is a demo I made under Web XR API which allow you run VR/AR/MR in a mobile phone from a webpage without downloading any additional package or App. It’s also easy to impliment a whiteboard into the scene like this one:
https://codepen.io/michaelsboost/pen/cnCAL

So this is another idea but it’s more practical than the big data one.

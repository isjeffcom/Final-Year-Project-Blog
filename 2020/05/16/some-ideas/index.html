Nonsense

Political

Despite the democracy system(or modern political system) whether globally or been practised in most of the counties, is created to end the bloody war of power. We seem to live in a peaceful world after the World War II. However, is that truly fix everything or we are in another cycle of history and everything just a delusion. Consider most people can only see a small window of the timeline we currently excited, we everything we see and think is just all bias.

What is the true relationship between the ruling class and the general public? Down to the very base question: Who created power? What is power?

Technology

I am watching the Westworld TV series recently, and it describes a society that dominated by an AI which use an algorithm to predict every single person’s move, therefore to governing the human race and a ‘perfect’ world. However, the main idea in this show is that human have no freedom in this kind of society, but everyone’s life was planned. When the hero destroys the AI, a war beginning, the world looks like hell. So it’s truly only freedom or chaos that we can choose? Is the algorithm the final solution of all? What is freedom?

Privacy

How to decided what is privacy and what is not? For example, a person walking on a street and somebody took a photo with him/her (but he/she are not the main object) and for most of the cases, the photographer was not violating the law. And people can still recognize the person. So why a high-resolution camera pick-up a human face in a public area, and send it back to a server to run facial recognization application that is violating privacy?

The law said it depends on how this camera owner going to use it. But face that sort of data that everyone can see it on any public area. Should they really be protected? How horrifying these surveillance technologies can do? What is the way we can alert people? Or it is already happening but we just do not know? I used to work in one of the largest tech company, it is really quite concerning for some application in used and people have no idea….

Like another TV Series Person of Interest, same writer as the Westworld by Jonathan Nolan. The show illustrated a man who built an AI to defend national security by using massive surveillance feed provided by the government. In this case, he uses this system to protect human life and national security. Of cause later in this show also introduced the bad guys who want to misuse this system…and…do many standard dark side staff.

Many people believe that using AI system to enhance massive surveillance can protect public safety, especially when they met the delivery package was stolen or terrorist attacks. Many countries are building a massive surveillance system like the US in the name of national security, and many of them don’t have a long history valuing civil right, so what consequence could that produce?

Like I always said, imagine if you piss off police officer or his/her child, and he/she wants to retaliate. How easy could that be if the government has such a big power to watch on everyone?

Can all these above merges with data visualization? Who knows.

Initial Roadmap

Thinking

I am struggling in create an idea for my final year project. It is not an easy thing. I have no idea what I should do now.

A specific area that I would like to explore in is data visualization which it has is been quite a popular topic recently. Whether from the general, digital art aspect or business aspect, the data visualization are getting attention through the development of big data, social network and massive surveillance technologies. Furthermore, data visualization start or continuing performing a vital role in these areas.

Take COVID-19 outbreak as an example, whether government decision making, scientific research also the general public requires accurate data collecting and digital visualizing implementation. The governments, media, academy institutions and many third-party have made many digital products for visualizing the COVID-19 data.

Like me, for instance, I am personally made a website to deliver COVID-19 data in the UK targeting the general public https://covid19uk.live. And the government did keep improving their own data visualization methods. This dashboard for instance: https://coronavirus.data.gov.uk/

I have some ideas related to many different aspects like merge social network data or surveillance data to do visualization and somehow remain, people, how important to protect their digital privacy. For instance, I’ve made an Emotion Map in the Design with Data course by scraped images from social network and use AI facial recognition technology to read the facial expression, and eventually concluded an emotion score for an area. (https://playground.eca.ed.ac.uk/~s1888009/ef/#/map)

Indeed, artificial intelligence is also a great topic to talk about regarding the rapid development of this technology, which I am recently taking an online course related to that.

Roadmap

Well, because I have no specific idea for now and I think I need a way to somehow manage a brainstorm for that so:

Stage 1: Research, involve consider topics or ideas in political, economic, culture and technology aspect. For this stage, it is important to produce some sort of manifesto and general concept for doing further studies.

Stage 2: Idea, which I should have a general completed idea which can be explained in one or two sentences.

Stage 3: Feasibility Testing, which I shell test if the concept is doable.

Stage 4: Low-Fidelity Prototype, which to produce a basic prototype like design draft, UX flow or blueprints.

and so on… it’s the general staff like design, development, audience research and feedback etc…
